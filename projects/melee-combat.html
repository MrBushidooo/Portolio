Melee Combat System

Modular third-person combat with combo attacks, parry mechanics, and EQS-driven enemy AI — inspired by Sekiro and Ghost of Tsushima.

Genre
Third-Person RPG / Combat
Platform
PC (Windows)
Engine
Unreal Engine 5.2
Tools
Blueprints, Behavior Trees, Niagara VFX system
▶ Play Demo

Project Goal

My goal with this project was to design and develop a modular, responsive, and immersive third-person melee combat system featuring proper collision handling, flexible damage system, dynamic enemy AIs and visual/audio feedback for the player. Inspired by games like Sekiro, Ghost of Tsushima, I wanted to create a smooth system that felt satisfying to play and scalable enough to support a full RPG.

Combat Mechanics

Light & Heavy Attack Combos

Chained animations using montage-based combo logic. Players can switch between light and heavy attacks mid-chain.

Directional Attacks

Attacks respond to player movement direction and aim for more dynamic, readable combat.

Hit Detection

Precise weapon collision using custom trace logic for accurate, responsive hit registration.

Blocking System

Negates incoming damage depending on stamina level and enemy attack type.

Parry System

Parry window controlled via anim notifies. Timed parries trigger hit-stun animations and open enemies to counterattacks.

Damage System

Built around a custom DamageComponent and DamageInterface — attachable to any actor for consistent damage handling with per-actor customisation. Both Player and Enemy implement IDamageInterface for shared calls with their own response logic.

Interface Functions

  • TakeDamage() — Accepts incoming damage, triggers hit reactions and VFX
  • Heal() — Restores health up to the actor's maximum
  • SetMaxHealth / SetCurrentHealth — Blueprint or runtime stat setup
  • Die / OnDeath — Actor-specific death behavior
  • GetCurrentHealth, IsDead — Status queries for UI, combat logic, and AI

Enemy AI

  • Fast Attackers — Strike quickly and unpredictably, forcing faster player reactions
  • Strafing Enemies — Actively reposition using EQS before engaging
  • Boss Enemies — Heavy-hitting with larger hitboxes and aggressive AI logic

AI States

Idle · Attack · Frozen (Dazed/Parried) · Strafe

AI Controller

Manages Behavior Tree execution and converts Perception System updates into Blackboard changes for state transitions.

Behavior Trees

Each enemy class has a unique Behavior Tree driving state switching in response to Blackboard changes.

AI Perception

Sight-based player detection. Sends perception updates to the AI Controller for state transitions.

EQS

Scores environment points based on distance and visibility for dynamic enemy strafing and positioning.

Visual & Audio Feedback

VFX (Niagara)

  • Weapon trails on fast swings to convey motion and power
  • Hit impact effects — sparks and blood bursts on hit events
  • Sharp flashes and burst effects on parry and block

SFX

  • Different swoosh sounds for light and heavy attacks
  • Varying impact sounds depending on hit area
  • Sharp metallic audio cues on successful parry and block
